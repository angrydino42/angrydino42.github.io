The Holotypic Occlupanid Research Group : A Database of Synthetic Taxonomy This website is dedicated to cataloguing different kinds of plastic bag neck sealers, or "Occlupanids". The taxonomic structure mirrors the kind used in biological taxonomy, and though this site is primarily for fun, it's a huge help for conservationists in the field to identify how to remove a stuck Occlupanid from an animal.
