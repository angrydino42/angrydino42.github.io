EffectColor is a tag that can be applied in the init of the tile. Sign tiles are an example of what use this tag, and are labeled as either "EffectColor A" (255,0,255) or "EffectColor B" (0,255,255).
These tiles require a larger width, applied similar to "rnd", for the colour intensity to be specified, where black (0,0,0) is fully opaque and white (255,255,255) is fully transparent.


bftiles are "Buffer" tiles. these cells are not considered part of the tile's geometry, but rather act as extra drawing space.
